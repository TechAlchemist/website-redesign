High Security Cylinder Locks

You can rest assured that whatever you've stored with us is protected by a high security cylinder lock. Why? Because a bolt cutter can be used to slice the latch around a disk lock or padlock; and a disk lock with a standard key can be picked. A cylinder lock inserts directly into a door. There’s no vulnerable shackle or slide bolt to cut. From our point of view, the cylinder lock provides the additional level of security that translates into “no break-ins.”
